IBM Qiskit SummerJam Hackathon

Have you heard of a quantum computer? Do you want to try programming on an actual quantum hardware, with no background required?

DuQIS is hosting the week-long IBM Qiskit Community SummerJam Hackathon (North Carolina) from June 24th (Wed) to July 1st (Wed)! It is designed to be over a week so that participants would be able to take care of their other responsibilities in the meantime such as internships, research, summer classes, etc. You will have the opportunity to work on various projects of your choosing (like making a quantum game) or design your own. Check out some project inspirations here.

All the projects will be developed in Qiskit which is a language based on Python, and you will have the opportunity to run your circuits on the actual IBM quantum hardware!! If you are new to the whole quantum computing thing, don't worry! The friendly people from the IBM Qiskit community are happy to assist and help at anytime. Oh, did I mention that the winning team gets a prize of $500?
